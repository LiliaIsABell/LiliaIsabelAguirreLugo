Stan Stopper

During the winter of 2022, I made the art for the game Stan Stopper. It is an arcade style game in which you play as a bodyguard who needs to protect the idol from having fangirls disrupt his performance. With the help of a t-shirt cannon, the player must shoot the fans before they reach the idol and push them off the stage.
