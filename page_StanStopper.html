Stan Stopper

During the winter of 2022, I made the art for the game Stan Stopper. It is an arcade style game in which you play as a bodyguard who needs to protect the idol from having fangirls disrupt his performance. With the help of a t-shirt cannon, the player must shoot the fans before they reach the idol and push them off the stage. Bodyguard

This is the chracter the player plays as. I came up for a design for this chracter pretty quickly. However, because the game is themed around kpop, I looked up photos of specifically Korean bodyguards to see if they dressed a differently to American ones. Interestingly, there was no significant difference but I noticed that the bodyguards were either bald or kept their hair short, so I made sure to give this charcter a short and well coiffed hairstyle.
